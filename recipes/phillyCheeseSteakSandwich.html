Philly Cheesesteak Sandwich.


Description:

This Philly Cheesesteak is made with sirloin sliced into strips and seasoned with a delicious blend of herbs and spices for a flavorsome sandwich. Sometimes I toast the hoagie roll before topping it with the steak, caramelized onions, peppers, and cheese. You can find out more on Allrecipes.

Duration:

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 25 mins Total Time: 40 mins Servings: 4 Yeild: 2 Cheesesteaks

Ingredients:

  1. 3 ½ pounds vine-ripened tomatoes
  2. ½ teaspoon black pepper
  3. ½ teaspoon paprika
  4. ½ teaspoon chili powder
  5. ½ teaspoon onion powder
  6. ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  7. ½ teaspoon dried thyme
  8. ½ teaspoon dried marjoram
  9. ½ teaspoon dried basil
  10. 1 pound beef sirloin, cut into thin 2 inch strips
  11. 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  12. 1 onion, sliced
  13. 1 green bell pepper, julienned
  14. 3 ounces Swiss cheese, thinly sliced
  15. 4 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  3. Mix salt, pepper, paprika, chili powder, onion powder, garlic powder, thyme, marjoram, and basil together in a small bowl.
  5. Place steak in a large bowl; sprinkle seasoning mixture over top and stir to coat.
  7. Heat 1/2 of the oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add steak; cook and stir to the desired doneness. Transfer cooked steak to a plate.
  9. Heat the remaining oil in the skillet. Add onion and green pepper; cook and stir until tender and caramelized.
  11. Preheat the oven on the broiler setting. liide cooked beef between the bottoms of 4 rolls.
  13. Layer with onion and green pepper.
  15. Top with sliced cheese. Place on a cookie sheet.
  17. Broil in the preheated oven until cheese is melted.
  19. Cover with tops of rolls and serve.
